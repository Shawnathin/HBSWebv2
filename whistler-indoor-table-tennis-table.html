Whistler Indoor Table Tennis Table

Experience a true, competition-quality game with a consistently even surface and a reliable bounce across the table. Solid, stable, and refined in design, the Whistler pairs professional-level play with a clean look suited to modern interiors.

Indoor table Playback position EN-Norm 14468-1 Made in Germany

Delivery and setup support

  • Showroom confirms delivery timing and availability.
  • Playback position supports solo practice at home.
  • Installation can be added if you want the table ready to play.

Request a Quote Add to Cart

Best For

Crafted for indoor environments, the Whistler combines certified tournament performance with the freedom to practice and improve at home.

  • Indoor table, not weatherproof
  • For national and international tournament-level play
  • Can be used in playback position for solo practice

Tabletop

  • 25mm precision chipboard playing surface
  • Available in green or blue
  • Multi-coated surface with A-60mm powder-coated frame profile

Undercarriage

  • Compact type 4b undercarriage
  • Rectangle-tube profile 40 x 40 mm with powder-coated construction
  • 4 double wheels with height adjustment on outer legs up to 25mm

Net and Safety

  • Perfect-II-EN stationary compact net set
  • Height-adjustable cotton net with stable post and clamp
  • Single-handle release with automatic locking in storage and playing position
